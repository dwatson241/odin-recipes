Fresh Homemade Biscuits

If you have ever been home on a Sunday morning and wanted some fresh biscuits with your breakfast then this recipe is for you. Keep the ingredients in your pantry so that you are prepared for your next biscuit craving!

INGREDIENTs

  • 2 cups all-purpost flour (250g)
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 6 tbsp unsaltedbutter
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
